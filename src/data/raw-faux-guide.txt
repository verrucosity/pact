Whenever you get downtime, complete lobster, tuna, swordfish fishing tasks. They take longer than you'd expect, even with relics, but are worth good points

From here I suggest working on the Karamja easy diary (use lamp in prayer to get near the level for bone shards), explore your new unlocked zones, and work towards demonic pact unlocks/overhead prayers. Hunter contracts and blessed bone mining are both good for the 43 prayer grind. You can also safespot blue dragons in the Dragon Nest and break down the bones for bone shards. 

Focus on the strengths from your chosen relics when unsure what to do next. Good luck on your league grind, & wear your pink skirt proudly.
